About

The Managing Directors, Mr. Mohamed Pullisseri and Anwar Sadath Mundekkat had a strong ambition to start an Auditorium with all ultra modern facilities. Their dream comes true on December 2017 by the establishment of Crystal Plaza Auditorium at Melangadi, Kalpakancherry with facilities like A/C, 24 Hr Circuit camera, modern kitchen, spacious dining hall and auditorium, ample carparking facility, etc. This is the first auditorium with such modern facilities at Kalpakancherry. The Crystal Plaza is one of the largest and finest Auditoriums in Malappuram District providing over 400+ seats and spacious car parking.The Crystal Plaza Auditorium provides not only its facilities and equipments to clients, but expert service as well. It has a conference centre with a capacity for 404 people. In addition, there are parking spaces avaiable. Crystal Plaza amenities include rooms, car parking, etc. Our Party hall is a perfect place for celebrating your auspicious family functions and any type of functions such as Birthday party, Wedding Reception, Conferences, Get-together Partie, Office Parties, etc,. It is also an apt place for conducting Corporate Meetings, Seminars. Our charges are very reasonable and competitive. Crystal Plaza offers you unparalleled and supreme facilities. Stunning exteriors, mind blowing interiors which are well build, beautiful seating arrangements for more than 800 persons in two levels and 24 hours power supply makes Crystal Plaza Auditorium unique. We are the most prestigious Auditorium of Malappuram District. The 404 seater Auditorium with A/C has been a host to many Programs like Marriages, Get-together, Meetings, Orientation Classes.. The Auditorium building is an elegant structure with aesthetically designed Public Areas, Main Hall, Green Rooms, etc.

The Auditorium is strategically located in the Melangadi area of Kalpakancherry. The Auditorium is well connected by Bus services as well as the Tirur Railway Station which has its station just 20 7km distance from the Hall. The Auditorium has one of the finest stages in the City of with a width of 15 meters and a depth of 7 meters. The Auditorium stage is ideal for demanding theatrical performances. To enable quick change of scenes and sets as well as elaborate lighting design. The Auditorium has excellent acoustics. The Auditorium has a 24 hour costomer care service system to handle customer queries. In order to enhance security, the Auditorium has an elaborate CCTV System. The Auditorium is proud to be considered as one of the finest Halls in Kerala for presentation of prestigious Events.
